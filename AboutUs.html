About Us

Order Now - Nepal Service Provider

Order Now Pvt. Ltd. (Order Online Market) ordernow.com.np (अर्डर गरे भैहाल्यो नि!) is one of the largest trusty Nepal online store and open market platform which re-launched in Jan 1st, 2021. Originally Launched as Sabaibhanda Sasto "सबैभन्दा सस्तो" on March 3rd, 2020 during the Covid19 pandemic and serving the frightened Kathmandu Valley with doorstep delivery of essentials by following all the safety measures. With tremendous positive feedback from valuable customers, we are expanding the services arena of the company with the rebranding OrderNow. OrderNow is an outcome of an optimistic vision that visualizes creating a unique shopping marketplace for all genuine customers and merchants.

Our major intent is to establish order as the most preferred marketplace within and out of the country designating ourselves towards becoming a market-leading web-based sales port. One of the primary reasons we have the confidence to call ourselves a market-leading company or platform is due to the dedicated exemplary customer service that we provide to you. Along with the excellent services that we offer, we also provide you fascinating merchandise options at a reasonable price. We are in a constant thrive to gain and reach more and more loyal customers by providing prompt and respectful services. Even at the earliest stage, we have ranged of over 10,000s products from over 1000s sellers, 100s of brands, and over millions of customers using e-commerce is remarkable.

Moreover, we are dedicated to providing you prompt and quick services which make us stand out more than any other e-commerce site out there in the market. Other than that, it goes without saying that ordernow.com.np is the best place for all the merchants and buyers that are seeking a platform for the sale and buy of quality products.

Our primary focus is to promote the local products and manufacturers from national to international. Our attempt to bring the fresh vegetables from the farm to the table made an impact on customers that pushes us to bring the whole new dimension to it by Order Now.
